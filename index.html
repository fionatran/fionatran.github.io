I’m a human factors and UX designer creating and testing user-friendly interfaces, and helping clients comply with human performance regulatory standards. I currently work as a Human Factors Design Engineer at Medtronic. My work has been applied to medical devices, hospital workflows, and the energy industry. I have an MASc in Industrial Engineering (Human Factors) and BASc in Engineering Science from the University of Toronto.
