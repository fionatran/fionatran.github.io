Medical Device Programmer

August 2017—present

I am leading human factors and UX activities for an upcoming app that will allow clinicians to check and control settings for implantable medical devices. These activities include designing and prototoyping the user experience, analyzing use-related risks, and conducting formative and summative usability testing and design verification. Prior to this role, I was the human factors test lead for multiple releases of a similar app.

My goal is to ensure minimal safety risks to the patient and clinicians, and that the product complies with the IEC 62366 standard and FDA guidance on usability engineering for medical devices.
